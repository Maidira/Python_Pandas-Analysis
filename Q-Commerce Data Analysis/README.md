# Quick Commerce Dashboard 

An interactive analytics dashboard designed to analyze and compare performance across major quick-commerce platforms.

---

## Overview
This dashboard tracks operational and business performance for:

- Amazon Now
- Big Basket
- Blinkit
- Dunzo
- Flipkart Minutes
- Jio Mart
- Swiggy Instamart
- Zepto

---

## Key Metrics

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Orders | 950K |
| Total Revenue | 540M |
| Average Rating | 3.04 ⭐ |
| Average Delivery Time | 16.51 mins |

---

## Insights

### Orders Analysis
- Order volume is consistently distributed across all platforms (~118K each).
- Indicates balanced market competition and steady demand.

### Revenue Performance
- **Swiggy Instamart** generates the highest revenue (~75M).
- **Jio Mart** records the lowest revenue (~57M).

### Delivery Performance
- **Zepto** has the fastest average delivery time (~10 mins).
- **Jio Mart** has the slowest delivery time (~23 mins).

### Customer Ratings
- **Blinkit** has the highest customer rating (~3.5).
- **Dunzo** shows comparatively lower customer satisfaction (~2.5).
